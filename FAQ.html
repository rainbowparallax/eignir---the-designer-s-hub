Q. Are the samples royalty free?

Q. Is the process of sound design tough?
A. Yes and no. Any line of profession poses several challenges, but in the field of design, in order to be well-equipped with overcoming challenges, passion and creative thinking are essentials.
Q. Will I become a pro if I have your samples/assets?
A. Our website offers the bases for your development process, but remember, your work is yours alone, so feel free to steal, but steal with reason and purpose.
Q. Video Game Design vs. Game Developement?
A. Game Design includes the creation of new game concepts and storylines whereas game development focuses on the making of games from animation to engine programming.
Q. Is Game Designing competitive?
A. Indeed, it is a competitive business where you will have to prove yourself as one of the best.
